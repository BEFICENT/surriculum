MANAGEMENT UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAM (BAMAN)
  Diploma Area Advisor: Altuğ Tanaltay

Sabancı University's undergraduate program in Management recognizes the importance of well- educated and innovative managers for building organizations that compete successfully in global markets and contribute to the economic development of our country. The mission of this program is to foster individuals who will eventually become such managers. While the students learn current techniques and practices, the emphasis of the program is on the analytical and problem-solving skills that enhance the students' capabilities for innovative thinking and decision-making under changing conditions.

The undergraduate program in Management also provides sufficient opportunities to develop an academic background for those students who seek academic careers or wish to further their education in a specialized field.

The graduates of the program will be qualified for various types of positions in private, public and non-profit organizations.
