ENERGY MINOR UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAM (ENERG-MINOR)
  Diploma Area Advisor: Murat Kaya

The minor program extends far among faculties of SabancÄ± University to bring the multidisciplinary vision and structure of the University together in the subject of energy which has many aspects spanning many disciplines from social sciences to technology and innovation as well as politics. The program aims to equip our students with general aspects of energy sources, systems, technologies and policy issues to improve their leadership potential for the rapidly growing energy sector in Turkey.

Core Elective Courses
Minimum 15 SU credits must be taken from the pool below.

- At least one course must be taken from introductory courses pool. (ENRG 315, ENRG 401)

- At least one course must be taken from scientific fundamentals courses pool. (ENS 202, ENS 207)

- At least one course must be taken from social, political and economic fundamentals courses pool. ( ECON 204,SPS 384,ECON 323,SPS 374,ENRG 423 )

- At least one course must be taken from technology, systems and materials fundamentals courses pool. (ENRG 420, ENRG 322, MAT 204, MAT 309,ME 305, ME 307, ME 309, ME 420, ME 441, CHEM 405)
