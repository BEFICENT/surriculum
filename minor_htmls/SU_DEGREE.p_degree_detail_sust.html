SUSTAINABILITY MINOR UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAM (SUST-MINOR)
  Diploma Area Advisor: Ozan Duygulu

The proposed Minor Program in Sustainability is ideally aligned with Sabancı University’s mission and vision statements, as well as its strategic plan and research focus areas. Sabancı University’s vision of having a “transformative impact on science and society” is fully supported by this program, given that sustainability represents one of the most urgent and globally impactful transformations of our time. Additionally, the interdisciplinary structure of the minor— hosted across multiple faculties—strongly reflects the university's commitment to “developing and disseminatin knowledge and science interdisciplinary.”

Core Elective Courses

Minimum of 3 courses (9 SU credits) must be completed. Students are required to take at least one course from the 2 categories described below.

Category 1: Courses with codes IF, BIO, SPS are in Category 1.

Category 2: Courses with codes MGMT, ORG, SOC, CULT, IR PHIL are in Category 2.

Each additional core elective course taken (after completing the core elective degree requirements) may count as an area elective.
