PHILOSOPHY MINOR UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAM (PHIL-MINOR)
  Diploma Area Advisor: Ahmet Faik Kurtulmu≈ü
This program is designed for students who want to acquire a strong background in philosophy and develop their critical thinking skills. The program introduces students to the history of philosophy and contemporary philosophical debates, and equips them with the skills of interpretation and critical reasoning requisite for taking part in these debates. The program offers a sound formation that will prepare interested students for graduate study in philosophy and in humanities and social sciences.
