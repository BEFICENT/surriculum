ENTREPRENEURSHIP MINOR UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAM (ENTREP-MINOR)
  Diploma Area Advisor: Berna Beyhan
Entrepreneurship minor program will provide students with a certain level of structure and the ability by making use of theoretical and acting contributions made in recent years in the field of business administration. The program will ensure that students have knowledge and perspective in the major intellectual trends and debates in the areas of entrepreneurship, intrapreneurship and technology.
