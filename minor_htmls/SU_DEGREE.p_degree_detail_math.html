MATHEMATICS MINOR UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAM (MATH-MINOR)
  Diploma Area Advisor: Mohammad Sadek

The program targets students typically from engineering and economics programs who plan an academic or research oriented career. The Minor Program aims to give a mathematical background beyond standard undergraduate engineering or economics curriculum. With such a background, students not only will have a serious advantage in their graduate studies in mathematics but also gain the ability to evaluate the problems from analytical perspective.
